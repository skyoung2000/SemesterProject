Lack of Sleep is Associated with Mental Health Issues

It is no question that sleep affects people's daily lives. Getting little sleep makes a person feel fatigued and less energetic throughout the day.

Not getting the right amount of sleep affects not only your physical health, but also your mental health and abilities. Because of this, it is important that you get enough sleep each night to protect your overall well-being.

These studies have shown that typically, females get less sleep on average than males and that females typically have a higher rate of having mental health issues than males.

This information suggests that there is a link between amount of sleep and mental health, and that each of these issues affects adult women at a higher rate than adult men.
